Welcome to the RAIVN Lab

The Reasoning, AI and VisioN (RAIVN) Lab at Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Washington is directed by Prof. Ali Farhadi. Our research is at the intersection of Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing and Robotics and tries to help computer improve their perception and reasoning.

Previous Next

RAIVN Lab's famous research break-throughs include YOLO, XNor-Net, VCR, BiDAF, dogTorch among many other amazing projects.
